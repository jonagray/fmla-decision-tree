FMLA Leave Decision Tree

FMLA for Yourself

Have you been employed at King County for at least 12 months in the past 7 years?

Have you worked at least 1,250 hours in the past 12 months?

You may be eligible for FMLA leave for yourself. Contact TransitFMLA@kingcounty.gov to apply. Be sure to submit the required documents.

FMLA for a Family Member

Does your family member have a serious health condition?

Have you been employed at King County for at least 12 months in the past 7 years?

Have you worked at least 1,250 hours in the past 12 months?

You may be eligible for FMLA leave to care for a family member. Contact TransitFMLA@kingcounty.gov to apply. Be sure to submit the required documents.

FMLA for a Newborn

Are you the biological, adoptive, or foster parent of the newborn?

Have you been employed at King County for at least 12 months in the past 7 years?

Have you worked at least 1,250 hours in the past 12 months?

You may be eligible for FMLA leave to care for a newborn. Contact TransitFMLA@kingcounty.gov to apply. Be sure to submit the required documents.

You are not eligible for FMLA at this time. Please contact HR for further assistance.
